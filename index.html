About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at SUNY Stony Brook, specializing in computer vision, deep learning, and medical imaging. My research focuses on developing innovative machine learning solutions for healthcare applications, particularly in the areas of medical image analysis and diagnosis.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Jadavpur University with a GPA of 9.29/10. During my undergraduate studies, I gained extensive research experience through internships at prestigious institutions including the Indian Statistical Institute and IIT Kharagpur.
